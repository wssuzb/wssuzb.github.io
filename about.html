About Me

My name is Zhenbo Su (苏镇波), I was born and grew up in QuanZhou (泉州), FuJian (福建) province, located in the east-south of China, where the Maritime Silk Road Starts.

I’m a graduate student at Department of Astronomy, USTC now. My current interests focus on the time-domain astronomy, e.g., Active Galactic Nucleus (AGN), especially the Variability of AGN. Future, I would focus on developing the theoretical models and combining the multi-wavelength observation data to explore the physical structure and geometry of AGN.
